## Critical errors

### 1. LANGUAGE: Everything must be in Italian

The whole deck is in English except random Italian on the last slide. This meeting is between three Italians. The deck MUST be entirely in Italian. Not half and half. Not English slides with Italian notes. TUTTO IN ITALIANO.

Read the transcript at `/sessions/practical-tender-maxwell/mnt/tercier/Call with Marco Di Cesare.transcript.txt` to understand the register. Informal, direct, conversational. Not corporate Italian. Not "siamo lieti di presentarvi" garbage. Write like Marco talks.

### 2. WIFE SALARY: Remove immediately

Slide 11 says "Wife earns CHF 130K. This works." This is private information that has ZERO place in a business presentation. Remove any mention of Marco's wife, her salary, or household finances. The only salary info that matters is Marco's CHF 4K/mo and the escalation triggers.

### 3. MARKET SCOPE: This is NOT a Swiss-only play

The deck presents Tercier as a Swiss market company. It's not. Read the business plan carefully:

- Global TAM: 55-75K hotels, EUR 1.3-2.7B
- European SAM: 6-8K hotels, EUR 144-288M
- Swiss BEACHHEAD: 272 ICP hotels (this is where we START, not where we stay)
- By M18-24 the plan explicitly says Northern Italy + DACH expansion
- By M36 the plan targets 115-154 hotels, which is impossible in Switzerland alone (there are only 272 addressable)

The deck must present Switzerland as the launchpad. The opportunity is European, potentially global. Amedeo and Corsaro run JAKALA which operates in 20+ countries. They don't care about a Swiss-only business.

### 4. THE "9 DAYS" SLIDE IS TOO MUCH

Nobody cares that Marco worked for 9 days. The pipeline (2,069 -> 718 -> 272 -> 1,632 simulations) is impressive as a one-liner to establish credibility, not as a whole slide. Compress this into 2-3 bullet points on the intro or context slide. The work speaks for itself through the quality of the rest of the deck.

### 5. OPERATING PRINCIPLES ARE MISSING

The current deck barely mentions Marco's operating principles. They need their own slide. From the business plan:

4. **Clienti > Capitale.** 10 clienti dalla vostra rete valgono piu' di CHF 100K di investimento.

Write these in Marco's voice, Italian, short and punchy.

### 6. QUESTIONS FOR THEM ARE MISSING

This is a founding conversation, not a pitch to a VC. Marco needs to ASK things, not just present. The deck must include a slide (or section at the end) with Marco's open questions for Amedeo and Corsaro:
