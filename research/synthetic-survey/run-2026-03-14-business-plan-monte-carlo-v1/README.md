## Framing

This version is built to improve the business plan and financial model directly.
It is grounded in:
- the March 2026 business plan milestones
- the financial model assumptions
- the CHF 200K operator-seeded reality

Both paths are AI-native. The difference is not "old SaaS bootstrap vs VC".
The difference is:
- **Operator-seeded AI-native** = capital-efficient compounding with network distribution
- **VC-accelerated AI-native** = same model, but faster integrations, GTM, and portfolio expansion
