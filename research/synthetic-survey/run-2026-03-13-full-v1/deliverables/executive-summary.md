## Methodology Note

This is a **synthetic survey** using AI-simulated hotel buyer personas. Results are hypothesis-grade, not ground truth. The pipeline:
1. Loaded real hotel dossiers with website-derived enrichment
2. Simulated 3 interview replications per hotel per role (GM/Owner + Commercial/Marketing)
3. Aggregated replications using median/majority logic
4. Applied adversarial critique to challenge optimistic assumptions
5. Ran a stronger-model judge pass for canonical normalization
6. Merged dual-role perspectives with conservative bias

**Models:** Interview: gpt-5-mini, Adversarial: gpt-5-mini, Judge: gpt-5

### Caveats
- Synthetic respondents cannot capture actual internal politics, personal relationships, or real budget constraints
- Hotels with limited website data may have less grounded responses
- The adversarial pass adjusts for known biases but cannot eliminate all synthetic optimism
- These results should guide outreach prioritization, NOT replace actual sales conversations
- Validate top findings with 5-10 real hotel conversations before scaling outreach
